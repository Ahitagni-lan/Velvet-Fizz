The Founders

Engineered for Taste

It started in a hostel room, not a Michelin kitchen.

We are four First-Year B.Tech CSE students who realized that the "gourmet" world had a bug: it was inaccessible. Good food was either too expensive or too hard to make. We decided to patch that.

Swapping algorithms for artisanal recipes, we applied the same logic and precision from our code to our kitchen. We aren't just selling mixes; we are debugging the fine dining experience to make luxury accessible to every Indian household.

From the console to the cocktail glass—this is our revolution.
